# Kai Liu

Kai Liu received the B.E. degree in Automation from the School of Automation, Central South University, Changsha, China, in 2025. He is currently pursuing the M.S. degree in Software Engineering at Xi'an Jiaotong University.

His research interests include embodied intelligence, vision-language navigation, vision-language-action models, and computer vision.

## Selected Work

### LANDER-Nav

**Project showcase, first author**  
[Project page](projects/lander-nav/)

LANDER-Nav explores multi-granularity open-vocabulary navigation. It represents a natural-language navigation goal with target, anchor, context, and relation cues, so that an embodied agent can better reason about objects, landmarks, and stopping viewpoints.

### PhysArtGen

**Project showcase, second author**  
[Project page](projects/physartgen/)

PhysArtGen is a related research project on interactive articulated 3D assets for embodied simulation. I participated in the design of the physical-consistency pipeline, URDF optimization workflow, dataset construction, and experimental evaluation.

### A Comprehensive Review and Future Perspectives on Embodied AI Large Models

**Survey paper, second author**

This survey reviews Vision-Language-Action models and embodied AI large models, covering model architecture, multimodal fusion, action generation, simulation platforms, datasets, benchmarks, and future challenges. [CNKI article][cnki-embodied-ai-review]
